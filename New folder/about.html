About ReNewed Life Church

ReNewed Life Church is a place where you can come as you are. We just want to show love like our Father shows us through His Son, giving us a way to have a relationship with Him. We are a church of unity—when you visit, we want you to feel at home and leave better than you came. Our pastors are passionate about unity and love, so whether you come to our church or visit our website, we want you to feel loved no matter where you are.

Our pastor always teaches us to grow a personal relationship with GOD and JESUS, getting to know Him for ourselves so He can change us and prepare us for whenever we leave this earth—because nobody knows their time. We may not be the biggest church, but we love to serve GOD with all our hearts, minds, and souls. When you stop by ReNewed Life, it’s a place where you can let go and let GOD—a place where you can be free to laugh, have fun, and worship the JESUS way. BAM!

Our Vision

TO EQUIP GENERATIONS WITH THE LOVE AND TRUTH OF JESUS CHRIST TO TRANSFORM THE WORLD

Pastor Larry D Smith

Pastor Larry Smith is an energetic and caring leader at Renewed Life Church in Raleigh, North Carolina. He loves God and wants to share messages about love, hope, and change. Pastor Larry helps people use their faith in everyday life. He grew up in Raleigh and knew he wanted to be a minister when he was young. He knows the Bible well and likes to help people.

As the lead pastor at The Renewed Life Church, Pastor Larry uses his knowledge, honesty, and kindness to guide his church. His inspiring sermons, based on the Bible and connected to real life, encourage church members to deepen their connection with God and practice their faith every day.

Outside of his sermons, Pastor Larry is recognized for his sincere interest in people's welfare. He looks for chances to help the community—arranging events, leading volunteer projects, or offering support to those who need it. Pastor Larry shows the kindness and sympathy of Jesus in everything he does. He leads Renewed Life Church with modesty, honesty, and a strong desire to change lives through the teachings of Jesus, spreading Jesus' love and kindness to the community and beyond.

First Lady Tamecia Smith

Tamecia Smith is a dedicated speaker, mentor, and supporter of spiritual development and empowerment. She is committed to serving God and helping others, which makes her a source of inspiration in her community. Tamecia grew up in Raleigh, North Carolina, and found her purpose early in life, driven by her strong faith and desire to make a positive difference. She focused on deepening her spiritual connection through studying religious texts and prayer.

Tamecia has spent many years serving in different roles within her church, from leading youth groups to organizing community outreach programs that tackle social issues and promote togetherness. Under her guidance, she has positively impacted many people, providing advice, help, and hope when they needed it most. With a sincere wish to spread God's message of love and forgiveness, Lady Tamecia has become a skilled and lively speaker, drawing in listeners with her charming manner and deep understanding.

Her honesty and approachability connect with people of all ages, encouraging them to strengthen their faith and live meaningful lives. As the spouse of Pastor Larry Smith, a mother, and a leader in the community, Tamecia lives by the principles of faith, honesty, and strength. Her life shows the endless opportunities that come when someone follows God's plan and lets His light guide them. As the First Lady of The ReNewed Life Church, Lady Tamecia is committed to sharing the good news about hope, healing, and being saved. She does this with kindness and modesty, encouraging others to start a spiritual journey and to feel the life-changing love of God.
